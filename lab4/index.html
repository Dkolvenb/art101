Everyday Tasks

How to make a soft boiled egg.

Get a pot, skimmer, desired number of eggs, A medium sized bowl (should be able to fit the eggs inside), and ice

Fill your pot with water and place on a stove until the water is boiling

Turn the heat slightly down while placing the eggs gently into the pot

Turn the heat back up

Cook for around 6 min

Prepare an ice bath during this time by filling the bowl with water and ice up to about 3/4

Remove the eggs from the water using your skimmer

Place them in a the ice bath to stop them from cooking further

Peel the shell and enjoy

How to change a car’s oil.

Make sure you have the right tools for the process

Look up your car’s oil capacity (usually in quarts)

Purchase any tools you are missing along with preferred oil rating and correct oil filter

Park car on a level surface and let cool for an hour

Find your car’s oil sump and unscrew the plug

Once drained, unscrew the oil filter and let drain

Wipe areas around plug insert and filter insert with a rag

Screw in sump plug. Lubricate the “O” ring on filter and screw into its spot

Locate the oil sump cap on top of the engine bay and begin filling with oil

Replace cap and let the car run for about five minutes

How to make rice without a rice cooker.

Get a pot that will hold preferred amount of rice and water

Measure amount of rice desired

Wash rice under cold water, once for stickier rice, or more for less-sticky rice

Place the rice in the pot under ~2inches of water

Let the rice soak for about 15-20 min with the lid on

Boil the rice at high temperature for 12-15 min

Remove from heat and let sit for 15-20 minutes

Remove the lid and enjoy the yummy yummy rice in your tummy
